# Weekend Update

Check out upcoming opportunities to upskill and enjoy the sunshine.

## Next Week…

- March 3 | 5:30pm-6:30pm: Customer Discovery (virtual) led by Randall Noval, Starfysh Founder. This hands-on workshop gives you a repeatable process for customer discovery in 60 minutes. You'll work through three cycles of teaching and practice to walk away with: testable hypotheses about a real problem, a ready-to-use interview script, and a plan to reach your first conversation. Register via: [https://luma.com/sbaeec9q](https://theupskillinglabs.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=50d1209f136e4d4e6c9eb9346&id=07eff14de7&e=291e9e0afc)
- March 5 | 12:00pm-1:30pm: (in person) at MLK Memorial Library. Vibe Coding led by Lilah Schaeffer, AI Conultant. Take your Claude Code projects from prototype to production-ready. In this hands-on workshop, we'll walk through integrating the tools modern apps actually need. Register via: [https://luma.com/ewpm5xt3](https://theupskillinglabs.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=50d1209f136e4d4e6c9eb9346&id=55b9f3c877&e=291e9e0afc)
- March 5 | 1:00pm–5:00pm: [Cowork. Co-learn. Co-survive the job market](https://theupskillinglabs.us6.list-manage.com/track/click?u=50d1209f136e4d4e6c9eb9346&id=664860780d&e=291e9e0afc). MLK Memorial Library (DC Public Library). Work alongside Pods, Projects and The Upskilling Labs’ volunteers.
