# Mentor

As a Mentor, you bring significant experience in your field and support upskillers in ways that match your expertise and availability. Your contributions may include:

- Leading workshops that teach a skill, framework, or methodology you know well
- Hosting office hours where teams can bring specific questions, roadblocks, or decisions
- Providing targeted coaching to pods working through complex problems
- Sharing insights, feedback, and connections through our Slack community
Time Commitment: Variable (based on your availability)
