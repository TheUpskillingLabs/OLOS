# Health Systems

January–April 2026

## April: 16 Upskillers

The Health Systems build cycle came to a close on the [Upskilling Summit](https://mailchi.mp/624d8d3a4766/welcome-to-the-upskilling-labs-11008014?e=). 16 Upskillers showcased their work on the Care Clarity and Health Pass projects, and set the stage for the kick-off of the Energy and Climate build cycle.

The Upskillers are the stars, but they didn’t get there alone. Mentors and volunteers organized dozens of events, mentors held office hours, our very own DC Public Library welcomed them in beautiful spaces week after week, and an entire community cheered them on.
