# Web & Tools Development

Support the development and maintenance of The Upskilling Labs digital ecosystem, including our website, community tools, and any technology that supports the delivery of our programs. Your responsibilities may include:

- Building and updating website content, pages, and features to keep our online presence current and functional
- Managing and optimizing community platforms and tools used for communication, collaboration, and program delivery
- Troubleshooting technical issues and providing support to organizers and volunteers as needed
Time Commitment: 5–10 hours/month
