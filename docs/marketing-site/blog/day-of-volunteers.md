# Day-of Volunteers

As a Day-of Volunteer, you will be on the ground ensuring a smooth, welcoming experience for every participant. Your responsibilities may include:

- Arriving early to assist with room setup, including arranging seating, signage, materials, and A/V equipment
- Managing registration and check-in, welcoming attendees, hand directing guests to the right spaces
- Supporting organizers with any last-minute needs during the event
Time Commitment: 3–6 hours per event day
