# Grant Writing (Copy)

Research funding opportunities and draft grant applications to support the Labs' programming, tools, and community growth. Your responsibilities may include:

- Identifying and tracking grant opportunities from foundations, government agencies, and other funding sources aligned with our mission
- Researching eligibility requirements, deadlines, and application guidelines for prospective grants
- Drafting and editing grant proposals, narratives, and supporting materials in collaboration with the core team
Time Commitment: 5–10 hours/month (varies by grant cycle)
