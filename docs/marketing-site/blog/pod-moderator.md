# Pod Moderator

The Pod Moderator (Poderator) provides structure and supports teams during the build cycle. Your responsibilities may include:

- Maintaining clarity of purpose, healthy working dynamics, and steady progress within your Pod, without directing work, outcomes, or owning solutions
- Encouraging Upskillers to complete their weekly check-in forms consistently
- Communicating participant needs and feedback to other volunteers on the team
Time Commitment: 2 hours/week (via Slack and virtual meetings)
