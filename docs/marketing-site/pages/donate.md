### Frequently Asked Questions

- Is my contribution tax-deductible? Yes! The Upskilling Labs operates as a fiscally sponsored project of Superbloom, a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.
- Where does my contribution go? Your contribution helps cover the start-up costs for the organization, including but not limited to: Materials for our meetups, including signs and name tags
- Software subscriptions, such as Google Workspace, that allow us to operate as an organization
- Volunteer appreciation, such as pizza at our meetups
- Can I volunteer or get more involved? Yes! Go on over to the “Volunteer” page of our site. [Linked Here](/volunteer)
- Do you accept in-kind donations? We do. Please email hq@theupskillinglabs.org with more information about what you’re able to give.
