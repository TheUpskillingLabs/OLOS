# Our Why...

### Losing your footing is hard. Losing faith in the future is harder.

The systems we’ve relied on are breaking. Here you're invited to turn that disruption into resilience—rooted in your local library and powered by you and your neighbors.

Mission: To help professionals upskill in emerging technologies through hands-on, peer-powered, project-based learning delivered in a replicable model that civic communities across the country can adopt.

Vision: A country where anyone, in any community, can learn and apply emerging technologies through programming hosted with public institutions that serve as hubs for lifelong upskilling.

Purpose: Flood the zone with hopeful stories, durable skills, and civic strength.
